For Arduino Esplora, use the following pinout:

Esplora |   |WT588D 
-----|:-----:|:--:
*Name* | *right exp. header* | *module pin*|
MISO | 9     |  6 DO 
SCLK | 8     |  7 CLK 
MOSI | 7     |  5 DI 
CS/D7| 6     |  9 CS
VDD  | 2     | 16 VDD 
GND  | 1     |  8 GND
